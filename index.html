Titans Arena

Titans Arena is a mobile action game that immerses you in the world of mythological gods. In the ancient ages, partake in an epic battle among the gods who ruled the world. Confront gods inspired by Greek, Norse, Egyptian, and many other mythologies.

Your main character possesses sword-wielding skills and formidable elemental abilities. Utilize these powers to engage in battles against legions of enemy units and elevate your reputation. As your reputation soars, brace yourself for one-on-one duels against the gods themselves.

In Titans Arena, strategy and skill combine as you have the opportunity to collect equipment sets to customize your character. Strengthen your hero with unique equipment and devise distinctive tactics to overcome your adversaries.
